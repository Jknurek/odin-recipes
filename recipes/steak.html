Steak Recipe

Im going to share my technique for butter basting steak, with a few key principles that will have you searing like a boss in no time.

Ingredients

  • 1 Thick ribeye steak
  • Kosher salt
  • 3-4 tablespoons quality salted butter
  • 2 springs of fresh rosemary
  • 2 springs of fresh thyme
  • 3-4 peeled whole garlic cloves
